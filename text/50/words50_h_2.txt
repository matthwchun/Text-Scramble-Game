Leaping clowns happily toss rutilant scarves in the air wearing increasingly disheveled top-hats and dancing up a idiosyncratic hurricane, creating cacophonous chaos amidst the pulchritudinous tabernacle. The contortionists especially were polyphiloprogenitive such that any bisection of the troupe was consanguineous. The pizazz in the piazzas, a balm to sovereign existentialism.
